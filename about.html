About nextECA

For the Community, By the Community

What is nextECA?

nextECA is a free and open-source platform created by nextPrtnr to help students, creators, and changemakers discover local and global opportunities such as hackathons, competitions, olympiads, and more.

Why nextECA?

In a world full of amazing opportunities, students often ask, "What’s next?". nextECA solves this by curating and listing quality extra-curricular activities from verified sources, all in one place.

Built with ❤️ for Students

Whether you're looking for a challenge to grow your skills or a chance to shine on a national stage, nextECA is your trusted starting point. It’s maintained by a growing open-source community and always open to contributions and feedback.
